About Me

Maihong Xu

Data Analytics Professional

Hello, I’m Maihong

I’m a Business Analytics professional with a passion for transforming complex data into actionable insights. With my foundation in Economics and hands-on entrepreneurial experience, I bridge the gap between analytical thinking and practical business solutions.

My journey through data science began during my undergraduate studies when I discovered how powerful analytical tools could transform business decision-making. This fascination led me to pursue my MS in Business Analytics at Boston University, where I’ve been expanding my technical toolkit while developing strategic business acumen.

What drives me is the challenge of finding the story behind the numbers and creating value from seemingly disparate data points. Whether it’s optimizing marketing campaigns, streamlining supply chains, or enhancing customer experiences, I find fulfillment in delivering data-driven solutions that make a tangible impact.

My Professional Journey

2022-2024
The Entrepreneur

My entrepreneurial venture with an online beauty shop taught me the real-world application of data science. From implementing NLP models to analyze competitor reviews to designing RFM segmentation strategies that boosted customer retention, I experienced firsthand how analytics drives business growth. This journey shaped my understanding of the full business lifecycle and cemented my belief in data-driven decision making.

2022
The Digital Marketer

At Beijing Radio Cultural Transmission, I discovered my passion for digital audience engagement. By developing Python ETL pipelines and implementing clustering algorithms to segment 500K+ fans, I learned to translate raw engagement metrics into strategic marketing decisions. Seeing our hashtags climb to top rankings on Weibo and Douyin validated the power of data-backed content strategies.

2022
The Community Builder

My work with the Bridging Hope Charity Foundation revealed the human impact of organizational effectiveness. Leading mental health initiatives across communities showed me that solid program management and feedback mechanisms directly enhance human wellbeing. This experience reinforced my commitment to pursuing analytics work that ultimately improves people’s lives.
